Prayer Request Terms of Service

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

The Service

Redemption Church Seattle offers a prayer request line so that members, attendees, and visitors can share what's on their heart and have our prayer team pray for them. You can reach us by texting our published prayer line number, or by submitting a request through our website.

Text Messaging Consent

By texting your prayer request to our prayer line, you agree to receive:

  • An automatic reply confirming your request was received
  • Optionally, a personal reply from a member of our prayer team

Message frequency varies based on your request and our team's responses. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages, or HELP for help. Text REMOVE to have your prayer request data deleted from our records.

Appropriate Use

This service exists to receive genuine prayer requests. Please don't use it to send spam, harassing content, or unrelated solicitations. We reserve the right to stop responding to numbers that misuse the service.

No Guarantee of Immediate Response

Our prayer team reviews requests as they come in, but this is not an emergency service. If you are in crisis or danger, please contact 911 or the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

Privacy

Our handling of your information is described in our Privacy Policy, which is part of these terms.

Changes to These Terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Continued use of the prayer line after changes means you accept the updated terms.
